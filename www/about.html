About webserv

Welcome to Webserv & Sons, a proud friendly operation founded in the grand tradition of hardworking developers.

Legend says the project began generations ago, when our great-grandparent accidentally opened port 8080 while trying to warm up a loaf of bread on a UNIX machine. The neighbors connected. Requests were served. And thus, our humble HTTP dynasty was born.

Over the decades, we refined our craft with the same values passed down in every respectable family business:

  • Listen carefully (to sockets, not feelings)
  • Respond quickly (or at least before the evaluator loses patience)
  • Never leak (memory, secrets, or shame)
  • Keep it simple (C++98 simple… like it's 1998 and we scroll koreus.com)

Today, Webserv & Sons continues to deliver time-honored services: a small but hardworking web server that speaks HTTP, handles connections, obeys a configuration file, and pretends to be calm under pressure—just like the founding family at every holiday dinner.

Is it a massive industrial solution with cloud scalability and enterprise monitoring?
Absolutely not.

But it answers requests, serves pages, and keeps the legacy alive—one slightly panicked poll() at a time.

So grab a browser, knock politely with a GET request, and enjoy the warm, handmade experience of a server built with love, tradition, and a suspicious amount of caffeine.
